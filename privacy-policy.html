Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

At Sri Varahaswamy Guest House (operated by Task Bucks Travel & Concierge), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website or services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us through our enquiry forms and WhatsApp communications:

  • Personal Details: Name, contact number, and email address.
  • Stay Details: Check-in/check-out dates, number of guests, and room preferences.
  • Identity Proof: As per local regulations in Tirumala, valid government-issued ID details may be requested at the time of booking or check-in.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process and confirm your guest house reservations.
  • Communicate with you regarding your booking and provide concierge support.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements for accommodation in Tirumala.
  • Improve our website and customer service experience.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with:

  • The management of the guest house where you are staying to facilitate your check-in.
  • Legal authorities if required to comply with Indian law or TTD regulations.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Disclaimer Regarding TTD

Please note that this website is an independent travel agency platform. We are not the official TTD (Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams) portal. Any information you provide is given to our private agency to facilitate your stay.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@srivarahswamyguesthouses.in or via the phone number listed on our contact page.
