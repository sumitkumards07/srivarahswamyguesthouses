How to Reach

Sri Varahaswamy Guest House is easily accessible from Tirupati and major transport hubs.

🚗 By Road

Tirumala is well-connected by a well-maintained ghat road. RTC buses and private taxis are available 24/7 from Tirupati bus stand and railway station.

🚂 By Rail

The nearest railway station is Tirupati Main (TPTY). From there, it's a 1-hour drive (22km) to reach Tirumala hill top.

✈️ By Air

Tirupati Airport (TIR) at Renigunta is the closest airport, located about 40km from our guest house.
