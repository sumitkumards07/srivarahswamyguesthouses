Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

At Sri Varahaswamy Guest House (operated by Task Bucks Travel & Concierge), we understand that pilgrimage plans can change. This policy ensures clarity regarding cancellations and refunds.

Please Note: All room bookings are made through our private agency service. Your refund will be processed by our agency, not by TTD.

1. Cancellation Windows

  • 72 Hours Before Check-in: Full refund minus a minor processing fee (up to 5%).
  • 24 to 72 Hours Before Check-in: 50% refund of the total booking amount.
  • Less than 24 Hours Before Check-in: No refund will be provided.
  • No-Show: In case of a no-show, 100% of the booking amount will be forfeited.

2. Refund Processing

  • Timeline: Once a refund request is approved, the amount will be credited back to your original payment method (Bank account, UPI, etc.) within 7-10 working days.
  • Method: Refunds will be sent via the same payment channel used for booking.

3. Force Majeure

If TTD officially closes Tirumala or halts ghat road access due to emergencies (landslides, heavy weather, or security reasons), we will provide either a full refund or a free rescheduling option, subject to availability.

4. Modifications

If you wish to change your booking dates, please contact us at least 48 hours in advance. Modifications are subject to availability and may incur a change fee.

5. Contact Us

For all cancellation and refund requests, please message us on WhatsApp with your booking details and payment receipt.
